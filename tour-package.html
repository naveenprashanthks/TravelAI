Tour Description

Embark on an extraordinary journey through the majestic Himalayan landscape. This 10-day adventure takes you through ancient mountain trails, picturesque villages, and offers breathtaking views of the world's highest peaks. Experienced guides will lead you through this once-in-a-lifetime experience, ensuring your safety while immersing you in the local culture and natural beauty of the region.

Our carefully crafted itinerary balances challenging treks with relaxation time, cultural experiences with natural exploration, creating the perfect adventure for both seasoned trekkers and enthusiastic beginners with good fitness levels.

Detailed Itinerary

Day 1: Arrival in Kathmandu

Arrive at Tribhuvan International Airport where our representative will greet you. Transfer to your hotel in Kathmandu. Evening welcome dinner with trip briefing.

Day 2: Kathmandu to Lukla, Trek to Phakding

Early morning flight to Lukla (2,800m). Begin trek to Phakding (2,652m). 3-4 hours of easy walking along the Dudh Koshi River.

Day 3: Phakding to Namche Bazaar

Trek to Namche Bazaar (3,440m), the gateway to Everest. Cross suspension bridges over deep gorges. 5-6 hours of moderate trekking with significant altitude gain.

Day 4: Acclimatization Day in Namche Bazaar

Rest day for acclimatization. Optional hike to Everest View Hotel for panoramic mountain views. Visit the local market and Sherpa Culture Museum.

Days 5-9: Main Trekking Circuit

Continue the trek through various Sherpa villages, monasteries, and breathtaking landscapes. Reach maximum altitude of 5,545m at Kala Patthar for spectacular Everest views. Begin descent through rhododendron forests.

Day 10: Return to Kathmandu

Morning flight from Lukla to Kathmandu. Free time for souvenir shopping. Farewell dinner with cultural performance.
