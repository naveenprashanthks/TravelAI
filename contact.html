Frequently Asked Questions

How do I book a tour package?
You can book a tour package by browsing our Tour Packages page, selecting a package, and following the booking process. You can also contact us directly for assistance.
What payment methods do you accept?
We accept all major credit cards, PayPal, and bank transfers for bookings. All payments are processed securely.
Can I customize a tour package?
Yes, we offer customized tour packages to suit your preferences. Please contact us with your requirements, and we'll create a personalized itinerary for you.
What is your cancellation policy?
Our standard cancellation policy allows for full refunds if cancelled 30 days before departure. Cancellations within 30 days may be subject to partial refunds or credits for future trips.
